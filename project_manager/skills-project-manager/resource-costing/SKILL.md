# Resource Costing

> **Meta:** v1.0.0 | 22-02-2026

## Purpose

Skill for calculating project resource costs, including full-time employees (FTE), contractors, rates, overtime, and market benchmarks. Helps accurately estimate resource costs and manage project budget effectively.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When planning project budget
- When estimating resource costs
- When comparing internal and external resources
- When analyzing resource utilization efficiency
- When preparing financial reports

## Functions

### FTE Cost Calculation
Calculating full-time employee costs:
- Determining full cost of FTE
- Accounting for taxes and contributions
- Calculating vacation and sick leave costs
- Estimating training and development costs
- Accounting for infrastructure and equipment costs

### Contractor Rate Analysis
Analyzing contractor rates:
- Comparing market rates
- Estimating cost of external specialists
- Analyzing value for money
- Accounting for cost of managing external resources
- Comparing with internal costs

### Overtime Calculation
Calculating overtime:
- Estimating overtime costs
- Accounting for impact of overtime on productivity
- Calculating personnel retention costs
- Analyzing impact on project budget
- Forecasting overtime needs

### Market Benchmarking
Market benchmarking:
- Comparing with market rates
- Analyzing labor market trends
- Estimating competitiveness of rates
- Forecasting resource cost changes
- Optimizing cost structure

## Integration with System Analyst

- Accounting for System Analyst participation cost in project
- Estimating costs of analytical work
- Comparing cost of internal and external analysis
- Accounting for time for architectural decisions
- Estimating cost of technical analysis

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Calculating FTE Developer Cost
Cost components:
- Salary: 150,000 RUB/month
- Taxes and contributions: 35,250 RUB/month (23.5%)
- Vacation and sick leave: 12,500 RUB/month (1/12 of 150,000)
- Equipment: 8,333 RUB/month (100,000 RUB/year)
- Training: 4,167 RUB/month (50,000 RUB/year)

Total FTE cost: 210,250 RUB/month
Cost per hour: 1,265 RUB (at 167 hours per month)

### Example 2: Comparing Internal and External Resources
Internal resource (System Analyst):
- Monthly cost: 250,000 RUB
- Hourly rate: 1,500 RUB
- Availability: 80% (accounting for administrative workload)

External resource (contractor):
- Hourly rate: 2,500 RUB
- Monthly cost: 417,500 RUB (at 167 hours)
- Additional expenses: 50,000 RUB/month (management)

Comparison:
- Internal resource is cheaper by 167,500 RUB/month
- External resource is faster available
- Internal resource has project knowledge

### Example 3: Calculating Overtime
Scenario:
- 100 hours of overtime required per month
- Base rate: 1,500 RUB/hour
- Overtime premium: 1.5x

Calculation:
- Overtime cost: 1,500 × 1.5 × 100 = 225,000 RUB
- Impact on budget: increase by 15%
- Alternative: hiring temporary employee
