# Risk Assessment

> **Meta:** v1.0.0 | 22-02-2026

## Purpose

Skill for identifying, analyzing and assessing project risks. Includes creating risk matrices, determining probability and impact of risks, developing mitigation strategies and risk response plans.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- At project planning stage for initial risk assessment
- When new risks arise during project execution
- For regular review and update of risk register
- When needing to determine risk impact on schedule and budget

## Functions

### Risk Identification
Identifying potential risks:
- Technical risks
- Organizational risks
- Financial risks
- Resource-related risks
- External risks

### Risk Analysis
Analyzing identified risks:
- Assessing probability of occurrence
- Assessing potential impact
- Determining risk category
- Prioritizing risks
- Analyzing risk triggers

### Risk Matrix Creation
Creating risk matrix:
- Defining assessment scales
- Visualizing risks
- Classifying by levels
- Defining responsibility areas
- Setting thresholds for monitoring

### Mitigation Strategies
Developing mitigation strategies:
- Avoiding risks
- Transferring risks (outsourcing)
- Reducing probability/impact
- Planning responses
- Resource buffering

## Integration with System Analyst

- Receiving information about technical risks from System Analyst
- Analyzing architectural risks
- Accounting for technology recommendations
- Assessing impact of technical decisions on risks
- Validating mitigation strategies from technical perspective

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Risk Assessment for Web Application Development Project
Technical risks:
- Risk: Insufficient team qualification in new technologies
  - Probability: Medium
  - Impact: High
  - Strategy: Team training, hiring specialists

- Risk: Problems with external API integration
  - Probability: High
  - Impact: Medium
  - Strategy: Early prototyping, time buffer

Organizational risks:
- Risk: Changing requirements during development
  - Probability: High
  - Impact: High
  - Strategy: Clear change management process

### Example 2: Risk Assessment for ERP Implementation Project
Financial risks:
- Risk: Budget overrun due to integration complexity
  - Probability: Medium
  - Impact: High
  - Strategy: 15% budget reserve, phased implementation

External risks:
- Risk: Legislative changes during project
  - Probability: Low
  - Impact: High
  - Strategy: Monitoring changes, legal expertise
