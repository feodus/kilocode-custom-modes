# Brand Management

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for managing and developing a brand. Includes developing positioning, defining tone of voice, creating brand guidelines, and communication strategy.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When creating a new brand
- For rebranding
- When developing a communication strategy
- For creating guidelines
- When launching new products under the brand

## Functions

### Brand Development
- Defining mission and vision
- Creating brand values
- Positioning
- Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
- Brand archetype

### Tone of Voice
- Defining tone
- Lexicon and stylistics
- Communication examples
- Writing guidelines
- Prohibited formulations

### Brand Guidelines
- Logo and its usage
- Color palette
- Typography
- Visual style
- Usage examples

### Communication Strategy
- Communication channels
- Messages for different audiences
- Content plan
- Crisis scenarios

## Brand Elements

### Brand Pyramid

| Level | Element | Example |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Attributes | What is it? | Car |
| Identity | What does it look like? | Sporty, modern |
| Benefits | What does it give? | Comfort, status |
| Values | What does it believe in? | Innovation, quality |
| Mission | Why does it exist? | Make life better |

| Situation | Formal | Friendly | Playful |
|----------|--------|----------|---------|
| Greeting | Good day | Hello | Hey there! |
| Thanks | Thank you | Thanks | Thanks a bunch! |
| Apology | We apologize | Sorry | Oops, my bad |
| Goodbye | Goodbye | Bye | See you! |

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Positioning
```
Brand: EduTech - Online education

Mission: Make education accessible to everyone
Values: Innovation, openness, results

Positioning:
- For whom: Adults 25-45 years old
- Problem: No time for offline education
- Solution: Flexible online format
- USP: Study at convenient time without leaving work

Competitive advantage:
Personal mentor and job placement guarantee
```

### Example 2: Logo guidelines
```
Logo usage rules:

1. Minimum size: 30px height
2. Clear space: 10px from edge
3. Background: white or brand color
4. Prohibited:
   - Changing proportions
   - Using non-brand colors
   - Rotating the logo
   - Adding effects
```
