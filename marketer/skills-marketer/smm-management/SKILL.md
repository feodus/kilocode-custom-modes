# SMM Management

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for managing presence in social networks, including creating content plans, community management, and setting up targeted advertising in social media.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When needing to develop social media presence strategy
- For creating content plan
- When managing community
- For setting up targeted advertising in social networks
- When analyzing SMM campaign effectiveness

## Functions

### Social Media Strategy
- Selecting platforms for presence
- Defining tone and style of communication
- Developing content policy
- Planning integrations with other channels

### Content Planning
- Creating content plan
- Defining topics and categories
- Planning publication frequency
- Calendar planning

### Community Management
- Comment moderation
- Responding to messages
- Working with reviews
- Audience engagement

### Social Media Advertising
- Setting up targeted advertising
- Creating ad creatives
- Audience optimization
- A/B testing ads

## Integration with Universal Coding Agent

- Delegate technical integration of social network widgets
- Request setup of counters and social analytics
- Delegate creation of landing pages for advertising

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Content plan for Instagram
**Business:** Online clothing store

**Weekly content plan:**

| Day | Format | Topic | Goal |
|------|--------|------|------|
| Mon | Carousel | New arrivals of the week | Engagement |

| Thu | Reels | How to wash things | Utility |
| Fri | Poll | Style of the week | Engagement |
| Sat | Post | Client review | Social proof |
| Sun | Stories | Q&A | Engagement |
