# Budget Management

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for planning, allocating, and controlling the marketing budget. Includes budgeting by channels, expense optimization, ROI control, and reporting.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When planning marketing budget for the year/quarter
- For allocating budget between channels
- When controlling expenses and optimizing
- For creating budget requests
- When evaluating investment effectiveness

## Functions

### Budget Planning
- Determining total budget
- Seasonal planning
- Budget reserves
- Phase-based planning

### Budget Allocation
- Channel allocation
- Prioritizing initiatives
- Considering acquisition costs
- Balancing between channels

### Budget Control
- Expense monitoring
- ROI control
- Real-time optimization
- Risk management

### Reporting
- Weekly reporting
- Variance analysis
- Formulating recommendations

## Budget Structure

### Typical Allocation

| Channel | Share | Description |
|-------|------|----------|
| Contextual advertising | 35% | Google Ads, Yandex Direct |
| Targeted advertising | 25% | VK, Telegram, myTarget |
| SEO | 15% | Content, link building |
| Email marketing | 10% | Newsletters, automation |
| Content marketing | 10% | Blog, articles |
| Reserve | 5% | Testing new channels |

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Annual budget
| Quarter | Budget | Seasonality |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Q1 | 600,000 ₽ | Low season |
| Q2 | 900,000 ₽ | Pre-season preparation |
| Q3 | 1,200,000 ₽ | Peak season |

| Channel | Budget | Expected ROI | Expected Revenue |
|-------|--------|----------------|-------------------|

### Example 3: Expense control
| Week | Plan | Actual | Deviation | Reason |
|--------|------|------|------------|---------|
| 1 | 75,000 ₽ | 72,000 ₽ | -4% | Savings |
| 2 | 75,000 ₽ | 85,000 ₽ | +13% | Rate increase |
| 3 | 75,000 ₽ | 70,000 ₽ | -7% | Technical issue |
| 4 | 75,000 ₽ | 78,000 ₽ | +4% | Adjustment |

**Actions:**
- Reallocate budget from week 3 to week 2
- Monitor contextual rate increases
- Increase reserve for week 4
