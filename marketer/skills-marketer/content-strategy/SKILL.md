# Content Strategy

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for developing and implementing content strategy. Includes content planning, creating editorial calendar, defining topics and distribution channels.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When developing content strategy from scratch
- For planning content for a long period
- When creating a content plan
- For optimizing existing content
- When selecting distribution channels

## Functions

### Content Planning
- Defining target audience
- Selecting topics and categories
- Planning formats
- Creating content plan

### Editorial Calendar
- Planning publications
- Resource allocation
- Considering seasonality
- Coordinating with campaigns

### Content Distribution
- Channel selection
- Reposting strategy
- Content syndication
- Repurposing materials

### Content Audit
- Analyzing existing content
- Identifying gaps
- Optimization
- Updating materials

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Content plan for B2B SaaS
**Target audience:** CEOs and marketers of small businesses

| Topic | Format | Channel | Goal |
|------|--------|-------|------|
| 5 marketing mistakes | Post | LinkedIn | Engagement |
| How to increase conversion | Video | YouTube | Leads |
| Client case study | Article | Blog | SEO |
| Checklist for CEOs | Guide | Email | Leads |
| Tool overview | Post | Twitter | Expertise |

### Example 2: Content funnel
**Product:** Online course

| Stage | Content | Format | Goal |
|------|---------|--------|------|
| TOFU | Blog articles | Articles | Traffic |
| MOFU | Webinars | Video | Leads |
| BOFU | Cases | Video + text | Sale |

### Example 3: Topic cluster
**Topic:** Contextual advertising

```
Cluster: Contextual advertising
├── Core: "Contextual advertising" (high-frequency)
├── Secondary:
│   ├── "How to set up Yandex Direct"
│   ├── "Google Ads vs Yandex Direct"
│   └── "Mistakes in contextual advertising"
└── Long tail:
    ├── "Contextual advertising for small business"
    ├── "Cost per click in Yandex"
    └── "How to reduce CPC"
```
