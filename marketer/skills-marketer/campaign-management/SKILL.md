# Campaign Management

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for comprehensive management of advertising campaigns. Includes planning, launch, monitoring, and optimization of campaigns across all promotion channels.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When launching new advertising campaigns
- For managing multiple channels simultaneously
- When optimizing running campaigns
- For coordinating cross-channel campaigns
- When scaling successful campaigns

## Functions

### Campaign Planning
- Defining goals and KPIs
- Selecting promotion channels
- Developing strategy
- Creating media plan
- Setting budget

### Campaign Launch
- Setting up ad accounts
- Uploading creatives
- Setting up targeting
- Setting bids
- Launch and monitoring

### Campaign Optimization
- A/B testing
- Bid optimization
- Targeting adjustment
- Scaling
- Working with conversions

### Cross-Channel Coordination
- Message synchronization
- Cross-channel retargeting
- Attribution
- Unified dashboard

## Campaign Lifecycle Stages

### Campaign Phases

| Phase | Duration | Main Tasks |
------|--------------|-----------------|
| Pre-launch | 1-2 weeks | Preparation, setup |
| Launch | 3-7 days | Testing, launch |
| Growth | 2-4 weeks | Scaling |
| Maturity | 4-8 weeks | Optimization |
| Wind-down | 1 week | Completion, analysis |

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Campaign plan
```
Campaign: New product launch
Goal: 500 sales in 30 days
Budget: 500,000 ₽

Phase 1: Testing (week 1)
- Test 5 creatives
- Test 3 audiences
- Budget: 50,000 ₽

Phase 2: Scaling (weeks 2-3)
- Scale best combinations
- Budget: 300,000 ₽

Phase 3: Optimization (week 4)
- Conversion final push
- Budget: 150,000 ₽
```

### Example 2: Launch checklist
- [ ] KPIs defined
- [ ] Creatives prepared
- [ ] Landing pages set up
- [ ] UTM tags configured
- [ ] Call tracking set up
- [ ] Audiences prepared
- [ ] Goals set up in analytics
- [ ] Analytics verified

### Example 3: Real-time optimization
| Day | Problem | Solution | Result |
|------|----------|---------|-----------|
| 2 | Low CTR | Replace creative | CTR +0.5% |
| 4 | High CPC | Add negative keywords | CPC -20% |
| 7 | Low conversion | Change landing | Conversion +15% |
| 10 | Good results | Scale | Leads x3 |

### Example 4: Scaling
| Stage | Budget | Daily | Conversion |
|-------|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Test | 5,000 ₽ | 1,000 ₽ | 3.5% |
| Scale x3 | 15,000 ₽ | 3,000 ₽ | 3.2% |
| Scale x10 | 50,000 ₽ | 10,000 ₽ | 2.8% |

**Conclusion:** Scale up to 10x, CPC increases insignificantly.
