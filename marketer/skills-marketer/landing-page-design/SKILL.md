# Landing Page Design

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for designing and optimizing landing pages. Includes creating structure, writing selling texts, UX design, and conversion optimization.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When creating a new landing page
- For optimizing existing landing pages
- When conducting A/B testing
- For increasing conversion
- When launching advertising campaigns

## Functions

### Structure Design
- Defining blocks
- Creating sequence
- Wireframe development
- UX design

### Copywriting for Landing Pages
- Headlines and subheadlines
- Offers and benefits
- Social proof
- Calls to action

### Conversion Optimization
- A/B testing
- Behavior analysis
- Form optimization
- Handling objections

### Technical Requirements
- Loading speed
- Responsiveness
- Integrations
- Analytics

## Sales Landing Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section
- Headline (H1)
- Subheadline
- CTA button
- Visual (photo/video)

### 2. Social Proof
- Client logos
- Reviews
- Cases
- Certificates

### 3. Problem-Agitation
- Target audience pain point
- How product solves it
- Benefits

### 4. Features
- Feature list
- Advantages
- Unique offerings

### 5. Pricing
- Tariffs
- Comparison
- Guarantees

### 6. CTA Section
- Final call
- Lead capture form
- Contacts

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Landing page structure for SaaS product
```
Landing page: Website builder

├── Hero
│   ├── H1: "Create a website in 15 minutes without code"
│   ├── Subheadline: "68 templates, free hosting"
│   └── CTA: "Create for free"
│
├── Social proof
│   ├── "10,000+ websites created"
│   ├── Rating 4.9/5
│   └── Client review
│
├── Problem-Solution
│   ├── Pain: "Website needed but no time/money for development"
│   └── Solution: Our builder solves in 15 minutes
│
├── Benefits
│   ├── Free for 14 days
│   ├── No coding skills needed
│   └── Your own domain for free
│
├── Pricing
│   ├── Free: 0₽
│   ├── Business: 990₽/month
│   └── VIP: 2,990₽/month
│
└── CTA
    └── Form: "Start for free"
```
