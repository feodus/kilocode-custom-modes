# Market Research

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for conducting market research. Includes target audience analysis, demand research, trend identification, and market opportunity assessment.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When entering a new market
- For defining target audience
- When launching a new product
- For understanding customer needs
- When researching trends

- Audience segmentation
- Creating personas
- Behavior analysis
- Customer journey maps

### Demand Analysis

- Demand dynamics
- Seasonality
- PEST analysis

### Trend Analysis
- Trend identification
- Forecasting
- Innovation analysis
- Technological trends

### Competitive Intelligence
