# Copywriting

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for creating selling and informational texts. Includes writing texts for websites, advertisements, email newsletters, and other marketing content.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When creating content for websites
- For writing advertisements
- When creating email letters
- For developing landing page texts
- When writing posts for social networks

## Functions

### Website Copy
- Home page
- About us
- Product/service cards
- FAQ
- Checklists and guides

### Ad Copy
- Contextual advertising texts
- Social media posts
- Banner ads
- Video scripts

### Email Copy
- Email subjects
- Email body
- CTA buttons
- Welcome series

### Landing Page Copy
- Headlines (H1-H3)
- Offers
- Benefits
- Calls to action

## Selling Text Formulas

### AIDA
- **A**ttention
- **I**nterest
- **D**esire
- **A**ction

### PAS
- **P**roblem
- **A**gitation
- **S**olution

### 4U
- **U**nique
- **U**sefulness
- **U**rgency
- **U**ltra-specific

## Integration

Coordinates with:
- Designers for visualization
- SMM for social networks
- Email marketers for newsletters

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Landing page copy for SaaS
**Product:** Website builder

**Headline:**
"Create a professional website in 15 minutes — without coding"

**Subheadline:**
"68 templates, 50+ integrations, free hosting. Try for free."

**Benefits:**
- ✅ Website in 15 minutes — no need to wait for weeks
- ✅ 68 ready-made templates — choose design in 1 click
- ✅ Built-in SEO — your site will be found on Google

**CTA:**
[Create website for free]

### Example 2: Email subject for ecommerce
**Goal:** Abandoned cart

**Option A:** "You forgot something in your cart 😢"
**Option B:** "Your order is waiting — 10% discount only today!"
**Option C:** "Ivan, your cart (order #12345)"

**Selected:** Option C (personalization + order number)
