# Competitor Analysis

> **Meta:** v1.1.0 | 08-03-2026

## Purpose

Skill for conducting competitor analysis. Includes monitoring, comparative analysis, identifying strengths and weaknesses of competitors, and developing positioning strategies.

## When to Use

Use this skill:
- When entering a new market
- For regular competitor monitoring
- When developing promotion strategy
- For identifying market opportunities
- When pricing positioning

## Functions

### Competitive Monitoring
- Activity tracking
- Change analysis
- Price monitoring
- Content analysis

### Comparative Analysis
- Product comparison
- Price analysis
- Marketing analysis
- Technological analysis

### SWOT Analysis
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Opportunities
- Threats

### Strategic Recommendations
- Positioning
- Competitive advantages

- Differentiation

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Competitor map
**Market:** SaaS for project management

| Parameter | Competitor A | Competitor B | Us |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Price | 29$/month | 49$/month | 39$/month |
| Features | Basic | Advanced | Medium |
| UX | Complex | Medium | Simple |
| Integrations | 50+ | 100+ | 30+ |
| Support | Chat | 24/7 | Email |

### Example 2: Competitor ad analysis
| Competitor | Budget | Channels | Main Offers |
|-----------|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Competitor A | 500K/month | Google, FB | Free period |
| Competitor B | 300K/month | Yandex, VK | 30% discount |
| Competitor C | 100K/month | SEO | Content marketing |

**Conclusion:** Niche is competitive. Recommendation: focus on email marketing and retention.

### Example 3: SWOT analysis
**Product:** Online programming courses

| Category | Factors |
|-----------|---------|
| **Strengths** | Strong brand, quality content, practitioners as experts |
| **Weaknesses** | High price, little practice, no job placement |
| **Opportunities** | AI assistant, job placement, corporate training |
| **Threats** | Free courses, competitors with job placement |
